Require controlled support-oriented reasons before teacher/parent access to protected adult support summaries and shared mood notes when policy requires it, without bypassing role/relationship/share authorization and with metadata-only audit for allowed, denied, and missing-reason attempts.

## Threat Model

| Threat | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Reason bypasses authorization | Role and active relationship checks remain mandatory before content returns. |
| Missing reason leaks content | HTTP 428 response includes only safe reason options; no protected content is rendered. |
| Invalid reason becomes free-text content | Only controlled codes from policy are accepted; no free-text reason input. |
| Audit leaks sensitive data | Audit metadata uses safe IDs/counts/codes only and existing sanitizer blocks forbidden keys. |
| Shared notes visible through summary reason only | Shared notes still require active `MoodNoteShare` and active relationship. |

## Success Criteria

- ACCESS-01 through ACCESS-05 are satisfied.
- Existing Phase 14 and Phase 23 support summary/share behavior remains intact when a valid reason is supplied or policy disables the reason gate.
- No SOS, adult notifications, external messages, risk scoring, background jobs, or free-text reason narratives are introduced.
